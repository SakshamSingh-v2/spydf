SPYDF

KEEP YOUR SCREENSHOTS ORGANIZED

Automatically convert your screenshots into PDF

HOW TO USE

1. Install and Start the program
2. Set your default screenshots path (only for first run)
3. Click start and take screenshots          (win+pr.scr)
4. Click Stop
5. Find your pdf in Documents/spydf folder
